# Limits & quotas

Each plan includes a monthly allowance of requests and bandwidth. Usage resets
at the start of each billing period.

## Plans

**Free** — for side projects and trying things out.

- 10,000 requests / month
- 5 GB bandwidth / month
- Unlimited projects

**Pro** — for production frontends with real traffic.

- 500,000 requests / month
- 500 GB bandwidth / month
- No hourly request limit
- Up to 6 MB per request

**Team** — for high-volume apps and businesses.

- 5,000,000+ requests / month
- 1 TB+ bandwidth / month
- Priority routing and support

See [pricing](/pricing) for the full comparison.

## How quota works

- **Requests** are counted per proxied call.
- **Bandwidth** is metered on the response body size. Because the proxy
  decompresses gzip/brotli responses in transit, bandwidth reflects the
  **decompressed** (logical) size, not the compressed wire size.
- When you exceed a quota, further requests return
  [`429 Too Many Requests`](/docs/errors) until the next period.
- `test_` keys carry an additional rate cap and aren't meant for production
  traffic.

Track your current usage any time in the [console](/console).

## Raising your limits

Upgrade your plan to raise quotas instantly — no need to rotate keys or change
code. Reach out via the [contact page](/contact) for Team and custom volumes.
